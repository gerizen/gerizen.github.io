What We Do.



Gerizen is involved in branding, marketing & distribution of various health products in India. Gerizen is also focusing for diversified global footprints by exporting its products to various countries and making a mark globally. We aim at improving the quality of life across communities where we work by respecting local cultures and engaging local people.

Breathing Pharma since 1975.



Our parent concern is our retail firm in the brand name of Ashok Pharmacy. The legacy of a famous, independent pharmacy continues for nearly half a century. We strive to improve ourselves everyday and aspire to be everyone's favourite health solution provider, with the highest standards of service excellence. Other business ventures include Laksh Mediline for pharma distribution and Vishal Foundation for real estate ventures.
